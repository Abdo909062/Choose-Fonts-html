Mo Salah

Mohamed Salah, also known as Mo Salah, is an Egyptian professional footballer who plays as a forward for Premier League club Liverpool and the Egypt national team. Known for his finishing, dribbling, and speed, Salah is considered one of the best players in the world.

Achievements

  • Premier League Golden Boot
  • CAF African Player of the Year
  • UEFA Champions League Winner
